Therapy Answers is a way to articulate your problems in a spoken or written form. By having to explain your problems, or answer prompts, you will gain a deeper understanding of the issue.

We cannot guarantee that our "answers" will help you. They are there moreso to help you when you get stuck, and inspire different paths of thinking.

If you feel uncomfortable talking out loud we recommend writing this out in your notes or thinking it through in your head. The process is what helps.

While working through your problems, it is best to remember the three C's:

 1. be calm - approach your problems with a level head

 2. be curious - ask yourself questions and don't forget to check in on how you feel

 3. be compassionate - show yourself concern and try to understand
